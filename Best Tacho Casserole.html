Best Taco Casserole

This hearty taco casserole brings all the bold flavors of tacos into one comforting dish, with layers of seasoned ground beef, crunchy tortilla chips, gooey cheese and zesty toppings, it's a crowd pleasing recipe that is simple to prepare. You can even substitute with ground turkey or low-fat diary for a lighter version without losing any of the flavor. Perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or game night, this casserole is best served with salsa, fresh chips and a crisp green salad.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef(or ground turkey)
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can(15 oz) refried beans
  • 1 can(10 oz) diced tomatoes with green chillies
  • 1 cup sour cream(or low-fat alternative)
  • 1 bag tortilla chips, lightly crushed
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup sliced Jalapeno's (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F(175°c).
  2. In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in taco seasoning, refried beans and diced tomatoes. Summer for about 5 minutes
  4. In a greased baking dish, spread a layer of crushed tortilla chips.
  5. Spoon the beef mixture over the chips
  6. Spread sour cream evenly over the beef layer.
  7. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese generously on top.
  8. Bake uncovered for 20-50 minutes, until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from oven oven, sprinkle with green onions, Jalapeno's and cilantro.
  10. Serve warm with salsa, extra chips and a green salad.
