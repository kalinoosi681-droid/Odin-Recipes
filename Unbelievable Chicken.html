Unbelievable Chicken

Unbelievable chicken lives up to it's name with a marinade that turns simple ingredients into an extraordinary dish. The unique flavor combination is both savory and tangy, creating juicy tender chicken impresses anyone who tries it. Perfect for grilling, baking or pan-searing, this versatile recipe is an instant favorite for family meals or backyard cookouts. Be prepared for endless compliments when you serve this dish.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together olive oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, parsley, garlic and black pepper until well combined.
  2. Place chicken breasts in a large resealable plastic bag or shallow dish. Pour marinade over the chicken, coating well.
  3. Seal or cover, then refrigerate for at least 30 minutes(up to overnight for best flavor).
  4. Preheat grill to medium-high heat(or oven to 375째F/190째c if baking).
  5. Remove chicken from marinade, discarding excess marinade.
  6. Grill Chicken for 6-8 minutes per side(or bake for 25-30minutes)until juices run clear and internal temperature reaches 165째F(74째c).
  7. Let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with sides like pasta salad,, rice or roasted veggies.
